Who should attend this event?

AI Day Bhopal 2.0 is for students, developers, engineers, and founders who want to build with AI — whether you're a beginner or already working in the field

Is this event beginner-friendly?

Yes, with both beginner and advanced sessions.

Is it a paid event?

Yes. Tickets are required to attend. Pricing includes access to sessions, workshops, lunch, and event swag

What is included in the Special Swag Pass?

Along with full event access, you’ll receive exclusive customized AI Day swag and a premium attendee experience

Will I get networking opportunities?

Yes. There will be dedicated networking sessions to connect with developers, founders, and AI enthusiasts
